Introduction

Overview

  • Dodgeball Battle is a game about use dodgeball to beat enemies with different strategies. Players are faced with different enemies who will always try their best to beat them, and changeable, interactive and sometimes complex landscapes. As a result, players need to utilize anything including environments, actions taken by enemies, and pickable items which can bring positive or negative buff to players to use dodgeballs beat all the enemies, and avoid being hit.
  • Dodgeball Battle is not only a simple sports game, actually it covers many different levels to support different mechanisms, including different enemies, different items, single playerâ€™s ability (PVE mode). This game will involve a series of levels for one or more players to beat a larger number of enemies, and the game design will inspire player(s) to use feasible strategies to pass the level.
  • Dodgeball Battle is not just a game with simple mechanisms. There are many games born with gorgeous blueprints and epic themes, costing much expenses, but not make players feel willing to play. We start with a brief idea, designing a game which brings fun to players and keeps interesting. Dodgeball Battle will be a game of great fun.

Premise

  • Dodgeball Battle is an action sports game. The players need to act like a dodgeball player, utilize environment and actions of opponents to avoid being hit by balls thrown by enemies and try to pick up balls to throw to hit the body of enemies. Several modes are offered to support different number of players.
  • This game is a pve game, the player will be put into environment and there will be mysterious attacks. The player needs to survive and get familiar with the environment and eventually pick up balls to crack the special mechanism and pass to the next level.

Key Feature

  • A picking up and throwing based core mechanic which is smooth enough to allow players to react fast.
  • Intensively various environment with pickable items with items and obstacles some of which are breakable.
  • Balanced, changeable and fun levels for cooperation & single-player mode (PVE).
